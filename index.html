About Us

About Cafe Biblio :

Cafe Biblio is one of the most iconic chains of coffee houses. It is one of the leading specialty coffee brands, known for its focus on high-quality, freshly roasted coffee sourced from Indian coffee farms. It has become a popular destination for coffee enthusiasts in cities like Hyderabad, Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and more. Literally a place where people can enjoy the atmosphere, have a sip of coffee,bit of cookies and enjoy reading books.

Cafe Biblio Trivia

When was Cafe Biblio established ?

Cafe Biblio was founded in the year 2020 by Chanikya with the aim of promoting Indian Coffee and elevating the coffee experience in the country.Cafe Biblio is widely credited with being one of the pioneers of the third wave coffee movement in India, bringing a culture of specialty coffee to urban cities. It is atmosphere surrounded with a cafe under library where you can enjoy the sip of coffee by reading variety of articles and story books. That's the moto of our tagline - blend of beans and books.
